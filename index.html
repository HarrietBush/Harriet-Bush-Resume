Kai ora, My name is Harriet Bush and I am a second-year student-athlete attending the University of California, Riverside. I am also an international student from New Zealand and I hope to spread the “Kiwi Story” to help connect Kiwi businesses to the American market. I believe that this exceptional education in America will help me gain connections and understand the American culture more so I am able t connect with suppliers across the country. That Is why I believe I would be an asset to your company.

New Zealand is a small island nation, with very unique flora, fauna, and rich cultural history. We are the home to the Maori people and many plants and animals (especially birds) found nowhere else on the planet. We are also free of a lot of diseases and pests that contribute to maintaining our native species. Therefore, I would come with a differing perspective on the protection and preservation of living on an island in the Pacific, who is very vulnerable to the climate, reliant on our primary industries and trading with other nations.

I would bring different interpretations of my work and interactions due to my different struggles, priorities, and values. Due to our geographical position in the world, New Zealanders have had to become very self-reliant. I believe we are extremely practical and have great problem-solving abilities. Due to this upbringing, I think I can contribute to your business by my ‘give it a go’ and ‘hard-working’ attitude. Having someone from a different country requires each party to learn empathy, growing acceptance, tolerance and hopefully, everyone will realize they have more in common than they thought. Diversity makes our world a more colorful and beautiful place for all. I am willing to work hard and learn new skills to help me develop an exceptional employee.
